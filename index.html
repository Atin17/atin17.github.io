Active Research

Neuron–Astrocyte Associative Memory

Astrocyte‑inspired gating stabilizes co‑active patterns and enables composition at recall; reduces false recalls and boosts composite top‑1 on CIFAR‑10/ImageNet subsets.

  • Evaluation suite with ablations (no‑astro, no‑time, shuffled).
  • PyTorch codebase, config sweeps, Loihi‑ready hooks.

Memory Synthesis & Few‑shot Bridging

Compose novel “child” memories from parent primitives; zero‑shot recognition of unseen composites and rapid 1–5‑shot adaptation.

  • Pretrain on synthetic composites → adapt with sparse real exemplars.
  • Identified failure modes & mitigations (astro gate, bin‑width tuning).

Closed‑loop Rehab Controller

Continuous‑attractor SNN state map + assistance‑as‑needed control on the InMotion Bionik Arm; assistance ∝ (1−confidence).

  • Metrics: completion time, path deviation, assistance work, basin volume.

ALM Choice‑Selectivity in Recurrent PPO

Instrumented recurrent PPO with astro‑gating; track choice selectivity, replay, and coupling of policy/value to astro traces in tone‑response tasks.
